I. NOTATION
===========

Move Name                           Motion
Damage  Level  Ex-Co-Re             Motion Delay
Notes


Move Name
---------
Pretty self-explanatory; names mostly created by players.


Motion
------
f,b,d,u   joystick motions (forward, back, down, up); hold if capitalized
db...     diagonals (db,df,uf,ub); again, hold if capitalized
P,K,G     press punch, kick, guard or evade buttons, respectively
+         buttons must be pressed together
,         move separator
n         return joystick to neutral



Damage
------
Given in points, standardized to Wolf's giant swing throw being 100 points, not
the percentage of the lifebar.
~ denotes proximity damage (range-dependant)


Level
-----
H,M,L,G   attack levels; low, mid, high and ground respectively
Ht,Lt     throw levels; high- and low- respectively
Bt        back-throw


Ex-Co-Re
--------
Move's execution, coverage (hit detection) and recovery in frames. There are
30 frames per second. Note: for throws, only recovery time has been listed
("rec").
D: denotes ground recovery


Motion Delay
------------
Time allowed between motion presses, in frames (vide ex-co-re for explanation).


Notes
-----
uncounterable: cannot be countered by opponent if blocked
force stand: if hits, forces the character to stand up
G: the move can be done while holding Guard
